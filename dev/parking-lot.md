| 2026-07-21 | Generator amounts are not heavy-tailed, so the demo has no Pareto story | `ProceduralGenerator` draws invoice amounts from `Normal(amount_mean, amount_std)`. Fix is a log-normal amount distribution in E3's generator (~1 line), but it invalidates seed-42 fixtures that s4.2/s4.3/s4.4 tests pin, so it needs its own story. s4.5 implements the filter honestly and reports the real figure. |
